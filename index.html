Welcome to A:MExchange on GitHub.

Here you will find a collection of resources for the Animation:Master community.

Share and perfect community resources

The purpose of this site is two-fold. Firstly, resource are shared in an easy and accessible way. Secondly, shared resources are refined based on requests. For example, a character might be modeled with a basic rig/skeleton included but a request to have the character updated to include the 2001, Squetch Rig or another rig provided may be submitted. Every detail of the model and rig can then be refined until the issue at hand is resolved and the request closed.
